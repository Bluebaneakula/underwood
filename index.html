Hello and welcome to unforgotten underwood.

Underwood Colony is a sim inside the game SecondLife.

We are a role play sim that allows people to create their own role play based on some basic history of the sim we have set out for you.

More information about the sim itself and rules are available here on the site along with interactions you can do while not on the sim itself.

In time we will be adding aditional interactions and games on the sim so you can enjoy underwood colony even when away from your PC.

The colony itself is set into 10 primary areas.

You can view a map and history of each area on the MAP link above.

Each area has its own distinct theme and style.

There is a lot to discover in each area so exploration and investigation is always advised.

Many things on the sim work as you would expect in the real world so feel free to interact with items you come across while exploring, some may lead you on interesting quests.

^__^

Currently we are working towards a connection system to link the website here with special terminals that are located in the airpad and docking station area.

As part of the colonies 30th aniversary celebrations we expect these to be fully operational by mid cycle and we appreciate your patience dueing this time.

As part of the renovations you may notice several areas of the colony under maintenance, we apologise for any inconvience this may cause.

Work will be completed by mid cycle.
